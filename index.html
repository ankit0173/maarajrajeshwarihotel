Our Story & Heritage

Since Generations
🍛

"Yahan ruk ke khana na khao,
toh safar adhura hai!"

NH 46 pe Bhangarh Village, Shivpuri mein — Maa Rajrajeshwari Hotel & Dhaba woh jagah hai jahan har truck driver, musafir aur family ko ghar jaisa khana milta hai. Chahe raat ke 2 baje ho ya dopahar ki dhoop — yahan hamesha garam dal-roti aur kadak chai tayyar rehti hai.

🔥 24 Ghante Tandoor ☕ Kadak Chai 🚛 Truck Parking 📹 CCTV Secured

Proprietor Mr. Sarvan Dhakad ji aur Manager Mr. Rajkumar Dhakad ji ki dekhrekh mein, hum saalon se drivers ki lambi journeys ka saathi rahe hain. Subah ki pehli chai ho, raat ka last meal ho, ya bas ek break — aao, baitho, khao!

🙏

Mitti ka tandoor 24 ghante chalta hai, parking mein aapka 16-wheeler aaram se aa jaayega, CCTV se poora area secured hai — toh aap befikr hokar khao, aaram karo. Yeh sirf ek dhaba nahi hai bhai — yahan apnapan hai!

Traveler's Corner

More than just food — we're your complete highway pit-stop. Built for truckers, welcoming for everyone.

24/7 Service

2 AM or 2 PM — hot dal-roti and kadak chai are always ready for every driver on the road.

Truck & Vehicle Parking

Spacious parking for trucks, trailers, buses, cars, and two-wheelers. Safe and easy access.

Clean Washrooms

Well-maintained and hygienic washroom facilities available for all travelers.

Family Friendly

Non-Stop Chai
